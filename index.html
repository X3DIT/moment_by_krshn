The eye behind the lens

A designer who learned to see before learning to shoot

My design background didn't just give me composition skills — it gave me a language for reading visual power. When I first stood before a Theyyam performer, I didn't see a ritual. I saw a living editorial, centuries in the making.

That shift in perception drives every photograph. Each frame is a deliberate design decision — not a capture, but a construction of meaning.
